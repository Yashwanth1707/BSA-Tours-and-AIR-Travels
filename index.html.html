Explore India

Kerala Backwaters

Drift through the calm backwaters of Kerala on a traditional houseboat and enjoy the lush green landscapes around you. This package offers a perfect blend of relaxation and nature, with scenic views, authentic Kerala cuisine, and peaceful surroundings. Ideal for families, couples, or anyone seeking a serene getaway.

Mysore & Bangalore

Explore the cultural heritage of Mysore with its palaces, temples, and rich traditions, then experience the modern lifestyle and bustling city life of Bangalore. This package blends history with urban vibes, making it a perfect getaway for all kinds of travelers.

Tamil Nadu Heritage

Tamil Nadu is a land of ancient temples, classical dance, and vibrant traditions. From the architectural marvels of Madurai and Thanjavur to the serene beaches of Rameswaram and Kanyakumari, every corner tells a story. Add to it the cool hill stations like Ooty and Kodaikanal, and you have a perfect mix of heritage, spirituality, and nature.

Goa

Unwind on Goa’s golden beaches, enjoy thrilling water sports, and experience the lively nightlife that makes Goa unforgettable. From beach shacks and seafood delights to Portuguese heritage and serene sunsets, this package is perfect for both relaxation and adventure seekers.

Karnataka Wildlife

Explore Karnataka’s famous wildlife sanctuaries and national parks, home to tigers, elephants, and diverse bird species. From the dense forests of Bandipur and Nagarhole to the scenic beauty of Kabini and Dandeli, this package is perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Experience safaris, eco-stays, and the raw charm of the wilderness.

Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is a land of diverse experiences — from the sacred Tirupati temple and ancient Buddhist sites to the stunning coastline of Visakhapatnam and Araku Valley’s scenic hills. With vibrant culture, delicious cuisine, and historic charm, this package offers a perfect mix of spirituality, history, and natural beauty.

Hyderabad City

Hyderabad blends history and modernity with iconic landmarks like Charminar, Golconda Fort, and Chowmahalla Palace. Explore bustling bazaars, savor the world-famous Hyderabadi biryani, and enjoy the city’s IT hub and modern lifestyle. A perfect mix of culture, cuisine, and city vibes.

Global Adventures

China Highlights

Discover China’s iconic attractions — from the Great Wall and Forbidden City in Beijing to the Terracotta Warriors in Xi’an and the skyline of Shanghai. Cruise along the Li River, explore ancient temples, and experience vibrant local traditions. A journey that blends rich heritage with breathtaking landscapes and modern city life.

Japan Discovery

From the serene temples of Kyoto and the iconic Mount Fuji to the bustling streets of Tokyo, Japan offers a perfect mix of heritage and innovation. Discover cherry blossoms, bullet trains, sushi delights, and vibrant culture. A journey that’s peaceful, modern, and unforgettable.

USA East Coast

From the bustling streets of New York City and the historic monuments of Washington D.C. to the cultural charm of Boston and the scenic beauty of Niagara Falls, the East Coast offers it all. Experience world-famous attractions, diverse cuisines, and vibrant city life in one unforgettable journey.

Thailand Islands

Thailand offers the perfect mix of relaxation and adventure — from the golden temples of Bangkok and the bustling markets of Pattaya to the serene beaches of Phuket and Krabi. Enjoy exotic cuisine, water sports, and rich cultural experiences that make Thailand one of Asia’s top holiday destinations.

Europe Romance

Europe is a dream destination filled with history, art, and breathtaking landscapes. From the Eiffel Tower in Paris and gondola rides in Venice to the Swiss Alps and the cultural treasures of London, every city has its own story. A perfect blend of romance, adventure, and unforgettable memories.

Australia Wonders

Australia offers a perfect mix of adventure, wildlife, and modern attractions. Explore the iconic Sydney Opera House, the Great Barrier Reef, and the scenic landscapes of the Outback. From golden beaches and bustling cities to unique wildlife encounters, every moment in Australia is unforgettable.

Dubai Luxury

Dubai is the ultimate luxury destination, blending modern marvels with Arabian elegance. Stay in world-class hotels, shop at designer malls, and enjoy desert safaris, private yachts, and fine dining. From the Burj Khalifa’s breathtaking views to the charm of Dubai Marina, every moment is crafted for a lavish experience.
